Icons

Our icons follow a consistent and clear design for user understanding, including dark mode variations for accessibility. Each icon had separate variations for the user to know exactly where they are in the app, and what they should expect when navigating our app.

Navigation Bar

Our navigation bar includes round edges and consistent color contrast, accomplishing a simple yet stylish look as its featured for most of the users experience throughout the app. Using commonly used icons for ease of navigation, for example the classic “House” icon to signify the home page.

Cards and Carousels

Cards within our app include a consistent rounded edge, typography, and color styles. Each card is designed for its own specific purpose showcasing short descriptions, supporting images, and a heart button for the user to be able to save / “Like” any certain flower, bouquet, or shop.

The addition of the drop shadow with each card creates a more three dimensional feel as opposed to a flat looking page.

Images

With our main idea for Bloom Guide being an easy way to find the perfect flower for any occasion, making flowers the key component. Every flower is different so having as many options as possible is vital for any user’s wants and needs. Categorizing flowers and bouquets using colors, location, uses, plus more creates a large and inclusive index of options for the user to explore.

As images are a large portion of the app each image needs to be clear, no unimportant distracting colors, keeping the flowers centered and visible, ensuring inclusive backgrounds, and making sure images stay a consistent size.
